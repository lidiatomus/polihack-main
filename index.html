ABOUT US

Helix is a non-profit organization founded by a dedicated team of five students, focuses on supporting neurodivergent individuals. Committed to fostering an inclusive community, Helix offers a range of programs, including support networks, educational initiatives, and skill development opportunities. The organization actively engages with local communities, schools, and businesses to promote neurodiversity awareness. Helix's vision is to create a world where neurodivergent individuals are celebrated for their unique strengths, and the organization invites volunteers, sponsors, and partners to join them in making a positive impact.
